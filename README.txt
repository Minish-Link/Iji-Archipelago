WHAT'S RANDOMIZED:

	Goals (3):
- Defeating General Tor at the end of Sector X
- Reaching all 10 posters and then reaching the end of Sector Z
- Obtaining the Massacre weapon and then defeating General Tor

	Locations (116 Total):
- Reaching the end of each Sector
- Leveling up by collecting Nano. Can level up 5 times per sector, for a total of 50 levels. (Obtaining Stat points through Supercharges does not count as leveling up.)
- Both Jump Upgrades. Found in Sectors 2 and 5.
- Two Armor Upgrades. Found as early as Sectors 3 and 7, but can also be found in Sectors 8, 9, and X if missed earlier.
- Ribbons. One in each Sector. None of them logically require any items. Missing one causes subsequent ones to not appear on that save file.
- Posters. One in each Sector. Usually requires specific weapons and tricky setups.
- Supercharges. One in each Sector. Even trickier to find than the posters.
- Basic Nanoweapons. Can be found in various Sectors, with more powerful weapons showing up in later Sectors. Most of them require Tasen or Komato stat points.
- Advanced Nanoweapons. Obtained by combining weapons at Cyborg Stations. Requires the Crack stat, as well as Tasen and/or Komato stats.
- Banana Gun. Only found in Sector 9. requires all basic Nanoweapons.
- Secret Skill Logbooks. Logbooks that each explain one of Iji's hidden abilities: Tech, Retribution, and Nanofield Reboot. Can be found in Sectors 7, 8, and X respectively.
- Scrambler (Sector 6)
- Trapmine (Sector 8)
- Annihilator Beta Terminal (Sector 8)
- Maximum Charge Terminal

	Items (72 Total):
- Progressive Sector Access. 9 total. Lets the player progress to the next Sector. The player starts with access to Sector 1.
- Progressive Stats. 9 each of Health, Attack, Assimilate, Strength, Crack, Tasen, and Komato. Allows the player to spend an additional stat point on the respective stat. The player starts at level 1 in each stat, so if the player has, for example, 4 Progressive Health Stat items, they may level up their Health stat to a maximum of 5.

NOTE: Some of the locations, namely the posters and supercharges, have difficult and specific requirements to obtain. It is recommended to use the above game guide as you play if you are not wholly familiar with Iji's secrets.
